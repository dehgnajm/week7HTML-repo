Meet Najmeh Dehghanitafti, a highly accomplished English language teacher and scholar currently based in Pocatello, Idaho. Najmeh's impressive academic journey has taken her from her undergraduate studies in English Literature at Yazd University in Iran to a Master's degree in Applied Linguistics from the same institution. Currently pursuing her PhD in English and the Teaching of English at Idaho State University, Najmeh is expected to graduate in May 2024, and her dissertation topic is a Meta-analysis of Morphosyntactic Structure Transfer in Third Language Acquisition. She also has graduated from the Front-End web development program at Promineo Tech and gained web development skills such as JavaScript, CSS, React, HTML, Bootstrap, JQuery, AJAX, Git & GitHub, and Webpack, as well as strong statistical abilities in R, SPSS, and Excel. Najmeh's research interests focus on Second and Third Language Acquisition, Meta-analysis, Sociolinguistics, Narrative Inquiry, and Language Assessment. In addition to her academic accomplishments, Najmeh has extensive teaching experience at various levels, from lecturing at universities to tutoring writing to undergraduate students. She has also presented research papers at international conferences and has been awarded several honors and scholarships for her academic achievements.
